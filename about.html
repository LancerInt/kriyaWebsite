Inside Kriya

Science-led partners for residue-conscious agriculture

Headquartered in Tamil Nadu, Kriya develops certified organic pesticides, biostimulants, and substrates designed for global distribution. We combine microbial fermentation, botanical extraction, and formulation science to deliver market-ready inputs that meet international compliance standards.

Our Story

We manage every step—from sourcing natural actives to documentation—so distributors receive premium inputs with reliable supply, technical training, and launch support.

Mission

Transform nature-derived actives into scalable, compliant, and high-performing inputs.

Vision
