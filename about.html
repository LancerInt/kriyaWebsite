Our Story

Who We Are

Headquartered in Tamil Nadu, India, Kriya combines science-led research with reliable production to deliver premium, eco-friendly inputs to global partners.

Kriya Ltd develops and supplies certified organic pesticides, biostimulants, and substrates designed for residue-conscious agriculture. We blend microbial discovery, plant science, and advanced formulation to produce inputs that perform across crops and climates.

Our teams manage every step—from sourcing natural actives to regulatory documentation—so distributors receive products that are market-ready and aligned with international compliance standards.

Our Mission

To transform nature-derived actives into effective, compliant, and scalable inputs that enable growers to reduce chemical residues, improve soil health, and achieve sustainable yields.

Our Vision

A future where every farmer accesses high-performance crop care solutions that are safe for people, soil, and the planet—supported by trusted partnerships worldwide.

Why Organic?

Meeting Global Demand for Natural Inputs

Organic agriculture is a necessity as markets demand traceable, low-residue solutions that protect soil health while delivering yield.

Rising consumer awareness, tighter residue regulations, and climate resilience demands are transforming crop care. Kriya’s portfolio provides distributors with scientifically validated products that meet certification requirements and farmer expectations.
