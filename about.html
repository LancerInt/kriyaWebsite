Science-led, distributor-focused

Kriya Ltd develops certified organic pesticides, biostimulants, and substrates from its headquarters in Tamil Nadu, India. We combine microbial fermentation, botanical extraction, and advanced formulation science to deliver premium inputs that help global distributors capture the shift toward residue-conscious agriculture.
