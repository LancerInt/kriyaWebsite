Stewardship guidance for organic inputs

Kriya partners commit to using our crop inputs in line with registered labels, safety data, and local regulations. These guidelines outline how to transport, store, and apply our products so distributors can maintain premium quality from factory to field.

handshakeDistributor commitments

Applying Kriya products responsibly

Follow the label

Always adhere to approved dosage, dilution, and re-entry intervals listed on the technical data sheet.

Protect workers

Ensure operators use recommended PPE and observe buffer zones when spraying near sensitive areas.

Store correctly

Keep products in original packaging, away from direct sunlight, moisture, or incompatible agrochemicals.

Dispose responsibly

Rinse containers three times and follow local regulations for recycling or disposal of packaging.
