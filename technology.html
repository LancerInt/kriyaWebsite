Technology Stack

Three specialized platforms. One integrated biological innovation system.

BioDesk presents Kriya as a technology-led agricultural biology company. Our platform stack is designed to discover, build, protect, deliver, and activate biological performance across microbial and botanical systems. Wynn powers microbial science and process development. Karyo powers delivery and protection of microbial and botanical actives. Microvate powers precision activation for microbial systems that are designed to become functionally ready only at the point of use.

How the Stack Works

How the stack works

Each platform in the Kriya technology stack has a distinct role. Wynn is the upstream microbial science engine. Karyo is the active delivery and protection engine. Microvate is the biological readiness engine. Together, they create a clear innovation architecture for next-generation crop input systems.

Platform Architecture

Role of each platform

Wynn™

Scope: Microbial only

Discovers and builds microbial systems through intelligence, analytics, process engineering, stabilization, and validation.

Karyo™

Scope: Microbial and botanical

Protects, encapsulates, combines, and delivers one or more actives through structured agricultural delivery systems.

Microvate™

Scope: Microbial only

Controls the transition from protected storage state to functional biological readiness through point-of-use activation.
