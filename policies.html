Privacy Policy

Commitment to data protection

Learn how we collect, process, and safeguard personal information shared with Kriya Ltd.

We collect only the information necessary to provide requested services, respond to inquiries, or meet regulatory obligations. Ethical product stewardship

Our products must be stored, handled, and applied in accordance with local regulations, product labels, and safety data sheets.
