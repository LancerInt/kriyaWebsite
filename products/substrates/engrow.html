Product Overview

Organic seed coating substrate for uniform emergence

Engrow coats seed with a blend of natural bio-extracts, protective binders, and micronized carriers that support germination and early root development. The formulation improves seed-to-soil contact, reduces early damping-off risk, and accelerates seedling vigor. Engrow is residue-conscious and suitable for organic certification, giving distributors a high-value seed treatment solution for sustainable agriculture.

Dosage & Application

Engrow usage guidance

Seed Type Dosage Application Method Guidance
Cereals & pulses 5–8 g/kg seed Dry coating or slurry application Mix with small volume of water; dry seeds in shade before planting.
Vegetable seeds 6–10 g/kg seed Slurry coating Use natural binder for uniform coating; sow once surface is dry.
Nursery plugs / tray dipping 1.5–2.5 g/L slurry Dip seeds or seedlings prior to sowing Ensure gentle agitation for consistent suspension.

Adjust rates based on seed size and target coating thickness. Compatible with microbial inoculants after jar testing.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most microbial inoculants and micronutrient coatings.
  • Perform jar test before mixing with chemical seed treatments.
  • Maintain slurry pH between 6.0 and 7.5.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Store in dry, cool warehouse away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep bags sealed to prevent moisture uptake.
  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months.
  • Use treated seed within 30 days for optimal performance.
