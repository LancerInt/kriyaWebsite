Product Overview

Seaweed excellence for flowering, fruit set, and yield

Blooma is a concentrated seaweed biostimulant crafted from Ascophyllum nodosum and complementary kelp species. Gentle extraction preserves natural cytokinins, auxins, betaines, and polysaccharides that trigger flowering, enhance fruit set, and safeguard yield quality. Dual-format offerings cover liquid and WSP applications, enabling distributors to serve horticulture, plantation, and vegetable segments. Blooma’s consistency is validated through marker compound assays and viscosity checks, while global compliance documentation supports rapid registration.

Dosage & Dilution

Blooma dosage guidelines

Application Timing Formulation Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Pre-bloom spray SL 250–350 mL 1.25–1.75 mL Apply 7–10 days before flowering Ensure thorough coverage of buds and young leaves.
Fruit set support SL 300–500 mL 1.5–2.5 mL Repeat 10–14 days after bloom Combine with calcium or boron foliar feeds where appropriate.
Pre-bloom spray WSP 250–350 g 1.25–1.75 g Apply 7–10 days before flowering Premix as slurry to avoid clumping in tank.
Fruit set support WSP 300–500 g 1.5–2.5 g Repeat 10–14 days after bloom Use non-chlorinated water for optimal bioactivity.

*Dilution assumes 200 L spray volume per acre. Adjust for crop canopy and equipment.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar sprays in orchards, vineyards, and plantations.
  • Greenhouse and hydroponic flowering programs.
  • Vegetable fields requiring yield stability.
  • Floriculture operations for cut flowers and potted plants.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most micronutrient and biostimulant inputs.
  • Perform jar test before mixing with alkaline or copper formulations.
  • Ideal companion with calcium/boron foliar feeds during bloom.
  • Maintain solution pH between 5.5 and 7.0 for maximum activity.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months (SL) / 30 months (WSP).
  • Store in cool, dry warehouse; avoid prolonged heat.
  • Shake SL containers prior to dosing; reseal WSP after partial use.
  • Protect from freezing to preserve natural polysaccharides.
