Product Overview

High-propagule AMF inoculant for resilient roots

EnRhize™ is a mycorrhizal fungal inoculant designed to enhance root exploration and nutrient capture through symbiotic colonization. It is best used at planting or transplanting where root-zone establishment and phosphorus acquisition are critical.
