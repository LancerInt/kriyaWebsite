Product Overview

High-propagule AMF inoculant for resilient roots

Enrhize delivers arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi (AMF) with a guaranteed 1500 propagules per gram. The powdered formulation colonizes roots rapidly, extending the rhizosphere, improving nutrient and water uptake, and fortifying plants against stress. Proprietary carriers protect spores during storage and enable easy blending with compost, potting mixes, or seed coatings. Enrhize is ideal for distributors seeking a reliable, organic-compliant AMF product backed by Kriya’s fermentation, QC, and documentation support.

Dosage & Dilution

Enrhize application guidance

Application Method Dosage Carrier / Water* Application Interval Guidance
Soil incorporation 3–5 kg/acre Blend with compost or fine sand for even distribution At planting or pre-plant soil prep Apply near root zone and cover lightly with soil.
Transplant dip 5 g/L Prepare slurry with clean, non-chlorinated water At transplanting Dip roots for 10–15 seconds before planting.
Seed coating 5–10 g/kg seed Use sticker or water to adhere to seed surface Before sowing Allow treated seed to dry in shade prior to planting.
Potting mix enrichment 1–2 kg/m3 Blend uniformly through growing media At media preparation Ensure thorough mixing for consistent spore distribution.

*Avoid chlorinated or heated water above 35°C to protect spore viability.

science Compatibility

  • Avoid mixing with fungicides or fumigants in immediate application window.

  • Do not expose to extreme heat or UV during application.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing.
  • Store in cool, dry area between 10–25°C.
  • Keep container tightly sealed to prevent moisture uptake.
  • Avoid freezing or exposure to direct sunlight.
