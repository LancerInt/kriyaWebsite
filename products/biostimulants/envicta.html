Product Overview

Multifunctional biostimulant for soil and crop performance

Envicta combines humic acid, fulvic acid, and amino acids to improve soil structure, nutrient availability, and plant resilience. The formulation chelates nutrients, enhances root growth, and stimulates metabolic activity for higher yields. Available in SL and WSP formats, Envicta fits fertigation, foliar feeding, and soil application programs. With consistent quality and regulatory documentation, it enables distributors to offer a comprehensive soil health solution aligned with organic and conventional systems.

  • Foliar feeding during vegetative growth for nutrient uptake.

Dosage & Dilution

Envicta application guidelines

Application Formulation Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Soil drench / drip SL 500–800 mL 2.0–3.0 mL Every 3–4 weeks Inject into irrigation system; flush lines before and after.
Foliar feed SL 300–400 mL 1.5–2.0 mL Every 14–21 days Apply during cool hours; combine with micronutrients if desired.
Soil drench / broadcast WSP 500–1000 g 2.0–3.0 g Every 4 weeks Premix with water or compost for uniform distribution.
Seed treatment / in-furrow WSP 5–10 g/kg seed 1.5–2.0 g At planting Blend with talc or other carriers for even coating.

*Dilution assumes 200–250 L spray or irrigation volume per acre. Adjust to local practices.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Field crops, horticulture, and plantations.
  • Greenhouse and hydroponic nutrient programs.
  • Turf and landscape management.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most fertilizers, biostimulants, and biologicals.
  • Avoid mixing with strong acids or oxidizers.
  • Maintain solution pH between 5.5 and 7.5 for best performance.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months (SL) / 30 months (WSP).
  • Store in cool, dry warehouse away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep containers sealed; avoid freezing.
  • Agitate SL drums before decanting; reseal WSP bags after opening.
