Product Overview

Multifunctional biostimulant for soil and crop performance

Envicta™ is a humic, fulvic, and amino acid complex designed to improve nutrient efficiency, root development, and stress recovery. It is suitable for foliar and root-zone support where both carbon-based chelation and metabolic activation are valuable.

Mode of Action

Humic chemistry improves nutrient and water dynamics

Primary Benefits

Improves soil structure and plant vigor

  • Root growth stimulation and expanded nutrient access.
  • Nutrient-use efficiency through chelation and transport.
  • Stress recovery and strengthened crop resilience.
  • Chelated nutrient transport and soil-plant metabolic activation.
Target Use

Versatile soil and foliar applications

  • Soil drench or drip irrigation programs for row crops and horticulture.
  • Foliar feeding during vegetative growth for nutrient uptake.
  • Seed treatment or in-furrow application for early vigor.
  • Blending with fertilizers to increase efficiency and reduce leaching.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Field crops, horticulture, and plantations.
  • Greenhouse and hydroponic nutrient programs.
  • Turf and landscape management.

science Compatibility

  • Generally compatible with neutral-pH fertilizers and crop inputs.
  • Avoid highly alkaline or strongly acidic concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Typical shelf life: 18–24 months in the unopened original pack under cool, dry, shaded storage.
  • Store sealed in a clean, dry place away from heat, direct sunlight, and contamination.
