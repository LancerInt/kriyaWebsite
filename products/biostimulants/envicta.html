Product Overview

Multifunctional biostimulant for soil and crop performance

Envicta combines humic acid, fulvic acid, and amino acids to improve soil structure, nutrient availability, and plant resilience. The formulation chelates nutrients, enhances root growth, and stimulates metabolic activity for higher yields. Available in SC format, Envicta fits fertigation, foliar feeding, and soil application programs. With consistent quality and regulatory documentation, it enables distributors to offer a comprehensive soil health solution aligned with organic and conventional systems.

Primary Benefits

Improves soil structure and plant vigor

  • Enhances cation exchange capacity for better nutrient retention.
  • Increases root mass and nutrient absorption efficiency.
  • Stimulates microbial activity and soil organic matter cycling.
  • Boosts plant resilience against abiotic stress.
Target Use

Versatile soil and foliar applications

  • Soil drench or drip irrigation programs for row crops and horticulture.
  • Foliar feeding during vegetative growth for nutrient uptake.
  • Seed treatment or in-furrow application for early vigor.
  • Blending with fertilizers to increase efficiency and reduce leaching.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Field crops, horticulture, and plantations.
  • Greenhouse and hydroponic nutrient programs.
  • Turf and landscape management.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most fertilizers, biostimulants, and biologicals.
  • Avoid mixing with strong acids or oxidizers.
  • Maintain solution pH between 5.5 and 7.5 for best performance.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18–24 months (SC).
  • Store in cool, dry warehouse away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep containers sealed; avoid freezing.
  • Shake SC containers before use to ensure uniform suspension.
