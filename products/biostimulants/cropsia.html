Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar sprays for open-field and protected horticulture.
  • Drip or sprinkler fertigation programs for field crops.
  • Nurseries, greenhouse starts, and transplant dips.
  • High-value plantations requiring yield stability.

science Compatibility

  • Generally compatible with neutral-pH fertilizers and crop inputs.
  • Avoid highly alkaline or strongly acidic concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Typical shelf life: 18–24 months in the unopened original pack under cool, dry, shaded storage.
  • Store sealed in a clean, dry place away from heat, direct sunlight, and contamination.
  • Standardized to 4% plant extract complex.
