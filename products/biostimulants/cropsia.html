Product Overview

Botanical stimulants designed for resilient growth

Cropsia blends moringa phytohormones with Adhatoda vasica metabolites to energize plant metabolism. The formula delivers antioxidants, amino acids, and secondary metabolites that promote chlorophyll production, root growth, and stress recovery. Liquid and WSP versions dissolve instantly without residue, ensuring clean spray operations. Quality control verifies active marker compounds and low phytotoxicity, while packaging options cover bulk drums to sachets. Cropsia equips distributors with a versatile, plant-derived tool for boosting vigor across diverse crops and climates.

Dosage & Dilution

Cropsia dosage for SL and WSP

Application Timing Formulation Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Early vegetative SL 300–400 mL 1.5–2.0 mL Every 14 days Foliar spray to wet canopy uniformly.
Pre-flowering SL 400–500 mL 2.0–2.5 mL Repeat in 10–14 days Add non-ionic wetter for waxy leaves.
Vegetative boost WSP 300–400 g 1.5–2.0 g Every 14 days Premix as slurry before adding to tank.
Stress recovery WSP 400–500 g 2.0–2.5 g Repeat after 7–10 days Combine with amino acids for compounded response.

*Dilution assumes 200–250 L spray volume per acre. Adjust to canopy density and sprayer calibration.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar sprays for open-field and protected horticulture.
  • Drip or sprinkler fertigation programs for field crops.
  • Nurseries, greenhouse starts, and transplant dips.
  • High-value plantations requiring yield stability.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most fertilizers and biostimulants.
  • Perform jar test before mixing with strong alkaline inputs.
  • Combine with micronutrients or amino acids for enhanced effect.
  • Avoid mixing with copper or sulfur on sensitive foliage.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months (SL) / 30 months (WSP).
  • Store in cool, dry warehouse away from sunlight.
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent oxidation.
  • Shake SL drums before decanting; reseal WSP bags after partial use.
