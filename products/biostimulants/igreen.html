Product Overview

Microbial horsepower for nutrient release and soil vitality

IGreen is Kriya’s flagship microbial biostimulant, formulated to energize soils and support crops across climate zones. Each consortium blends nitrogen-fixers, phosphorus solubilizers, potassium mobilizers, and protective strains to address regional agronomic pressures. Fermentation is executed under GMP controls, preserving viability at 3×1010 CFU per mL or gram. Stabilizers deliver long shelf life without refrigeration, while spore suspensions stay uniform in fertigation tanks and drip systems. Registration dossiers, residue studies, and efficacy data ensure smooth introductions in regulated markets. IGreen equips distributors with a premium, science-led portfolio to lead soil health programs.

Primary Benefits

Built for nutrient mobilization and soil health

  • Solubilizes bound phosphorus and mobilizes potassium locked in clay and calcareous soils.
  • Fixes atmospheric nitrogen to reduce synthetic nitrogen dependency.
  • Produces phytohormones and siderophores that energize root development.
  • Suppresses soil-borne pathogens through competitive exclusion and metabolite release.
  • Improves soil organic matter cycling and microbial biodiversity.
Pests & Diseases Addressed

Biological suppression of key soil threats

  • Damps-off pathogens (Pythium, Rhizoctonia, Fusarium) in nursery and field settings.
  • Root lesion and root knot nematodes via rhizosphere colonization.
  • Foliar opportunists including mildews and blights when applied as foliar drench.
  • Secondary pests by strengthening plant resilience and metabolite production.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Soil drench and drip irrigation programs for vegetables and specialty crops.
  • Seed coating and transplant dips for cereals, oilseeds, and nurseries.
  • Fertigation through greenhouse, hydroponic, and shade-net systems.
  • Orchards and plantation crops requiring sustained nutrient support.

science Compatibility

  • Avoid tank mixing with broad-spectrum fungicides or bactericides.
  • Maintain solution pH between 6.0 and 7.0 to protect spore viability.
  • Compatible with humic substances, micronutrients, and most biocontrols.
  • Introduce via fertigation after flushing lines to remove residual chlorine.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18–24 months in controlled ambient conditions.
  • Store between 10–30°C away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
  • Do not freeze. Keep containers tightly sealed to preserve moisture balance.
  • Agitate SL containers before use; rehydrate WP packs with clean water.
