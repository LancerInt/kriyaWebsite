Product Overview

Plant adaptogens that keep crops productive

Orgocare is a concentrated blend of plant extracts engineered to help crops withstand abiotic stress such as heat, drought, salinity, and chemical injury. The SL formulation combines polyphenols, amino acids, and natural osmoprotectants that stabilize plant cells and accelerate recovery. Orgocare is ideal for distributors seeking a premium stress mitigation product with organic-aligned inputs, backed by Kriya’s regulatory and marketing support. It integrates seamlessly with foliar nutrition and IPM programs to protect yield integrity.

Dosage & Dilution

Orgocare stress management program

Application Scenario Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Preventative (before stress) 300–400 mL 1.5–2.0 mL 7 days before anticipated stress Spray in early morning or late afternoon.
Rescue (after stress) 400–500 mL 2.0–2.5 mL Repeat after 7–10 days if symptoms persist Combine with amino acid feeds for faster recovery.
Transplant support 300 mL 1.5 mL At transplanting; repeat after 10 days Apply as root drench around new transplants.

*Dilution assumes 200 L spray volume per acre. Adjust to canopy size and sprayer calibration.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar sprays and drip irrigation for high-value crops.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most foliar nutrients and biostimulants.
  • Avoid tank mixing with copper or sulfur without jar test.
  • Maintain spray solution pH between 5.5 and 7.0.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months.
  • Store between 10–30°C away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep container sealed; do not freeze.
  • Shake before use for uniform dispersion.
