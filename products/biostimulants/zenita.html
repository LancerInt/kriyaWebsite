Dosage & Dilution

Zenita application guidance

Application Stage Formulation Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Vegetative flush SL 300–500 mL 1.5–2.5 mL Every 14–21 days Apply as fine foliar spray covering both sides of leaves.
Stress recovery SL 400–500 mL 2.0–2.5 mL Repeat after 10 days if stress persists Combine with chelated micronutrients for faster rebound.
Vegetative flush WSP 300–500 g 1.5–2.5 g Every 14–21 days Pre-slurry powder before adding to spray tank.
Stress recovery WSP 350–500 g 2.0–2.5 g Repeat after 10 days Use non-chlorinated water to protect protein activity.

*Dilution assumes 200 L spray volume per acre. Adjust carrier volume for canopy size and sprayer output.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar programs in fruits, vegetables, and plantations.
  • Fertigation or drip injection for horticulture and high-value field crops.
  • Greenhouse nutrient programs for hydroponic or substrate systems.
  • Nurseries and ornamentals seeking premium foliage quality.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most micronutrients and biostimulants.
  • Avoid tank mixing with copper or sulfur on sensitive crops without jar test.
  • Maintain spray solution pH between 5.5 and 7.0.
  • Introduce last in tank when combined with emulsifiable concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months for SL, 30 months for WSP.
  • Store between 10–30°C away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent moisture uptake.
  • Do not freeze the SL format; agitate gently before use.
