Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Foliar programs in fruits, vegetables, and plantations.
  • Fertigation or drip injection for horticulture and high-value field crops.
  • Greenhouse nutrient programs for hydroponic or substrate systems.
  • Nurseries and ornamentals seeking premium foliage quality.

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most micronutrients and biostimulants.
  • Avoid tank mixing with copper or sulfur on sensitive crops without jar test.
  • Maintain spray solution pH between 5.5 and 7.0.
  • Introduce last in tank when combined with emulsifiable concentrates.

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18–24 months (WP) from manufacturing.
  • Store between 10–30°C away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent moisture uptake.
  • Agitate gently before use to ensure uniform suspension.
