Dosage & Dilution

Program-ready dosage plans

Spindura Plus – Spinosad 22.5% SC

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & field crops 5–8 fl oz (148–237 mL) 1.5–2.4 mL / L Every 5–7 days
Fruit, nut & plantation crops 8–10 fl oz (237–296 mL) 2.4–3.0 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Protected cultivation / greenhouse 4–6 fl oz (118–177 mL) 1.2–1.8 mL / L Every 5 days

Spindura Pro – Spinosad 2.5% SC

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & field crops 45–60 fl oz (1.33–1.78 L) 13.3–17.8 mL / L Every 5–7 days

Protected cultivation / greenhouse 36–48 fl oz (1.06–1.42 L) 10.6–14.2 mL / L Every 5 days

Spindura Rix – Spinosad Wettable Powder

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & field crops 0.7–1.1 kg (1.5–2.5 lb) 7–11 g / L Every 5–7 days
Fruit, nut & plantation crops 1.1–1.4 kg (2.5–3.0 lb) 11–14 g / L Every 7–10 days
Protected cultivation / greenhouse 0.5–0.7 kg (1.1–1.5 lb) 5–7 g / L Every 5 days

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust carrier volume to match local sprayer calibration.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, pulses, cereals, and cotton
  • Fruit, nut, and beverage crop plantations
  • Greenhouse, shade-net, and hydroponic systems
  • Nursery ornamentals and floriculture
  • Post-harvest dip treatments for transplants (dilute baths)

science Compatibility

  • Mixable with most biologicals, spreaders, and foliar feeds
  • Avoid tank mixes with strong alkaline materials; conduct jar test
  • Maintain spray solution between pH 5.0–7.0 for optimal stability
  • Introduce last in the tank after dispersible granules and wettable powders

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct sunlight
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent moisture ingress and caking
  • Agitate concentrates before decanting for uniform active distribution
