Product Overview

High-performance spinosad for premium horticulture

Spindura Plus is a natural-origin insecticidal formulation based on spinosad for control of thrips and lepidopteran larvae. It is best positioned for early larval stages, rapid feeding cessation, and rotation-based resistance management programs.

Mode of Action

Spinosad acts on nicotinic acetylcholine receptors and GABA-gated chloride channels, causing neuromuscular hyperexcitation, paralysis, and death

Dosage & Dilution

Program-ready dosage plans

Spindura Plus – Spinosad 25.2% SC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early larval or thrips infestation 60–80 mL/acre 120–150 L Foliar spray with fine droplet coverage Repeat every 7–10 days if needed
Moderate infestation 80–100 mL/acre 150–180 L Foliar spray targeting feeding sites and concealed plant parts Limit sequential use within rotation programs

Spindura Pro – Spinosad 2.5% SC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early larval or thrips infestation 400–500 mL/acre 120–150 L Foliar spray with full canopy penetration Repeat every 7–10 days if needed
Moderate infestation 500–600 mL/acre 150–180 L Foliar spray covering feeding zones and lower surfaces Limit sequential use within rotation programs

Spindura Rix – Spinosad 12% WP

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early larval or thrips infestation 100–125 g/acre 120–150 L Foliar spray after preparing a smooth suspension Repeat every 7–10 days if needed
Moderate infestation 125–150 g/acre 150–180 L Foliar spray with constant tank agitation Limit sequential use within rotation programs

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust carrier volume to match local sprayer calibration.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, pulses, cereals, and cotton
  • Fruit, nut, and beverage crop plantations
  • Greenhouse, shade-net, and hydroponic systems
  • Nursery ornamentals and floriculture
  • Post-harvest dip treatments for transplants (dilute baths)

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most neutral-pH spray inputs
  • Avoid strongly alkaline mixes and confirm with a jar test

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Typical shelf life: 24 months in the unopened original container under cool, dry, shaded storage
  • Store sealed in the original pack away from heat, direct sunlight, and moisture
