Product Overview

Paecilomyces lilacinus for root-knot nematodes and soil insects

EnCilo delivers Paecilomyces lilacinus (Purpureocillium lilacinum) at 1 × 109 CFU levels in OD and WP platforms. Carefully controlled fermentation and encapsulation ensure spore viability, while formulation chemistries prevent settling in fertigation and spray systems. EnCilo colonises the rhizosphere, parasitises nematode eggs, and complements botanical and microbial programs targeting soil pests. Complete documentation packages support registration and private-label programs for international distributors.

Dosage & Dilution

EnCilo application programs

EnCilo OD – Paecilomyces lilacinus 1 × 109 CFU/mL

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Drip irrigation / fertigations 600–800 mL 6–8 mL / L Every 15 days for 3 cycles
Soil drench around root zone 500–700 mL 5–7 mL / L Every 20 days
Seedling dip / transplant soak 300 mL in 100 L water 3 mL / L Single pre-plant treatment

EnCilo WP – Paecilomyces lilacinus 1 × 109 CFU/g

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Soil incorporation / broadcasting 1.0–1.5 kg 10–15 g / L Apply at planting and 30 days later
Nursery media mixing 1.0 kg per cubic meter 10 g / L for slurry During media preparation
Root dip before transplant 500 g in 100 L water 5 g / L Single pre-plant treatment

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust volume to local irrigation and drench equipment.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Drip-irrigated vegetables and fruits
  • Nursery trays and propagation houses
  • Field crops and plantations
  • Turf, golf courses, and landscaped areas
  • Soilless and protected cultivation systems

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most biofertilisers and botanical nematicides
  • Avoid tank mixing with fungicides containing carbendazim or copper
  • Maintain solution pH between 5.5–7.0
  • Use chlorine-free water where possible

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18 months
  • Store in a cool, dry environment away from sunlight
  • Keep WP packs sealed to prevent moisture ingress
  • Agitate OD drums prior to decanting
