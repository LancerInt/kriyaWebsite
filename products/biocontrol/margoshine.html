Product Overview

Clarified neem oil solutions engineered for global distributors

Margoshine packages clarified hydrophobic neem oil into distributor-ready programs that deliver insecticidal knockdown while respecting residue limits. Cold-pressed oil is deodorized and filtered to retain azadirachtin, salannin, and nimbin fractions essential to integrated pest management. Emulsifier and wetting systems produce uniform sprays on waxy foliage and greenhouse crops. Every batch is QC-tested for free fatty acid levels, clarity, and emulsion stability to secure shelf life across hot and humid climates. Regulatory dossiers cover OMRI-aligned certifications, residue data, and region-specific labels to accelerate approvals. Both EC and WP formats share unified SDS, marketing collateral, and pricing frameworks for straightforward portfolio integration.

Margoshine 70 EC – Neem Oil 70% EC

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & field crops 0.5–1.0 gal (1.9–3.8 L) 5–10 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Fruit, nut & plantation crops 1.0–2.0 gal (3.8–7.6 L) 10–20 mL / L Every 7–14 days
Protected cultivation / greenhouse 0.25–0.75 gal (0.95–2.8 L) 2.5–7.5 mL / L Every 5–7 days

Margoshine 35 WP – Neem Oil 35% WP

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & field crops 1.5–3.0 kg 10–20 g / L Every 7–10 days

*Rates reference EPA Reg. No. 96019-4 for clarified hydrophobic extract of neem oil. Wettable powder conversions maintain equivalent active ingredient delivery assuming 50–100 gallons (190–380 L) spray volume per acre.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, spices, pulses, and cotton
  • Fruit, nut, and plantation crops across tropical and temperate zones
  • Greenhouse, shade-net, and hydroponic systems
  • Nursery ornamentals and propagation units
  • Post-harvest dips for transplant trays and seedlings

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most biologicals, spreaders, and foliar feeds
  • Avoid tank mixes with strong sulfur or copper within 3 days
  • Maintain spray solution between pH 5.5–7.0 for optimal stability
  • Introduce last in the tank after dispersible granules and wettable powders

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct sunlight
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent oxidation and moisture ingress
  • Agitate EC drums and tumble WP bags before dispensing for uniformity
