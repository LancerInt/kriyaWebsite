Product Overview

Metarhizium anisopliae solutions for soil insects and borers

Rexora pairs high-density Metarhizium anisopliae spores with engineered carriers to tackle root-zone pests without compromising residue programs. Built for international distributors, the EC formulation targets foliar-feeding and boring insects, while the WP format excels in soil and seed treatments. Each batch undergoes viability, moisture, and particle-size verification so distributors receive reliable, shelf-stable stock supported by comprehensive dossiers, toxicology data, and export-ready labelling.

Dosage & Dilution

Rexora application guidance

Rexora EC – Metarhizium anisopliae 1×10¹⁰ cfu/mL

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Soil drench / root zone 600–800 mL 6–8 mL / L Repeat every 10–14 days
Foliar sprays on plantations 500–700 mL 5–7 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Seed treatment / root dip 400 mL in 100 L water 4 mL / L Single pre-plant treatment

Rexora WP – Metarhizium anisopliae 1×10¹⁰ cfu/g

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Soil drench / drip irrigation 0.75–1.0 kg 7.5–10 g / L Repeat every 15 days
Nursery and potting media 0.5 kg mixed in media 5 g / L when used as slurry Apply at transplanting
Foliar spray (suspension) 0.6–0.8 kg 6–8 g / L Every 7–10 days

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust carrier volume based on local sprayer calibration.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Soil drench in plantations and orchards
  • Seedling trays and nursery beds
  • Field crop furrow and band applications
  • Golf greens and turf
  • Greenhouse vegetables and ornamentals

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most microbial inoculants and botanical insecticides
  • Avoid mixing with fungicides containing copper or triazoles
  • Maintain pH between 5.5–7.5
  • Use within 24 hours after preparing suspensions

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18 months
  • Store in a cool, dry location away from sunlight
  • Keep WP packs sealed to prevent moisture uptake
  • Agitate EC drums before decanting
