Product Overview

Engineered azadirachtin solutions for premium horticulture

Azadirachtin functions primarily as a feeding deterrent and insect growth regulator. It disrupts ecdysone- and juvenile-hormone-mediated endocrine signaling, interferes with molting and metamorphosis, suppresses larval feeding, and reduces fecundity, oviposition, and egg viability in exposed pest populations.

Dosage & Dilution

Program-ready dosage plans

Ecoza Max – Azadirachtin 3.0% EC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early infestation or preventive canopy spray 250–300 mL/acre 150–200 L Foliar spray with thorough canopy coverage Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays
Moderate pest pressure 300–400 mL/acre 180–220 L Foliar spray targeting the undersides of leaves Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays

Ecoza Ace – Azadirachtin 1.2% EC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early infestation or preventive canopy spray 500–750 mL/acre 150–200 L Foliar spray with uniform canopy coverage Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays
Moderate pest pressure 750–1000 mL/acre 180–220 L Foliar spray focusing on new growth and the lower leaf surface Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays

Ecoza Rix – Azadirachtin 1.2% WP

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early infestation or preventive spray 400–600 g/acre 150–200 L Foliar spray after preparing a smooth suspension Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays
Moderate pest pressure 600–800 g/acre 180–220 L Foliar spray with constant agitation in the spray tank Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays

Ecoza Pro – Azadirachtin 0.3% EC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Preventive or early infestation spray 1.0–1.5 L/acre 150–220 L Foliar spray with full plant coverage Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays
Sustained pest activity 1.5–2.0 L/acre 180–250 L Foliar spray repeated across the active flush period Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sequential sprays

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust carrier volume to match local sprayer calibration.
