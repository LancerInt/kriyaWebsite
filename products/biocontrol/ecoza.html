infoProduct overview

Residue-conscious azadirachtin technology

Ecoza formulations combine cold-processed neem actives with premium solvents and surfactants to deliver consistent azadirachtin content. The line covers high-strength EC concentrates for export crops, mid-strength EC for broad acreage, wettable powders for tablets, and 0.3% programs tailored for consumer packs. Each batch is standardized for azadirachtin A+B content, UV stability, and shelf-life under tropical conditions.
