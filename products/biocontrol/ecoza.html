Product Overview

Engineered azadirachtin solutions for premium horticulture

Ecoza is Kriya’s flagship azadirachtin platform, developed for distributors who require consistent, residue-conscious control of sucking and chewing pests. Each concentration is produced through cold extraction and micro-emulsification to preserve the full limonoid spectrum. Carrier systems are optimized for rapid dilution, tank stability, and even canopy coverage. Formulations remain flowable in warm and cool climates, safeguarding spray quality in diverse geographies. Ecoza integrates seamlessly with microbial and soft-chemistry partners to anchor sustainable IPM programs. Export-ready dossiers and traceability documentation accompany every batch.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, pulses, cereals, and cotton
  • Fruit, nut, and beverage crop plantations
  • Greenhouse, shade-net, and hydroponic systems
  • Nursery and ornamental production
  • Seed treatment and transplant dips (dilute baths)

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most biologicals, wetting agents, and low-pH fertilizers
  • Conduct jar test before mixing with copper or sulfur materials
  • Maintain spray solution between pH 5.5–7.0 for best stability
  • Add last in the tank after emulsifiable oils and dispersible granules

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct sunlight
  • Keep containers tightly sealed to prevent solvent evaporation
  • Agitate drums before decanting for uniform active distribution
