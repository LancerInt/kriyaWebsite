Azadirachtin portfolio

Ecoza – Botanical bioinsecticide for premium programs

Ecoza delivers residue-conscious pest management using high-purity azadirachtin sourced from cold-processed neem. Variants cover EC and WP formats for row crops, horticulture, and protected cultivation.

Cold-processed neem

Ensures consistent azadirachtin content for global registrations.

Portfolio flexibility

1.2% EC, 0.3% EC, and WP formats to fit different markets.

Regulatory support

Label data, dossiers, and EPA-aligned dosage guidance.

Product overview

Ecoza is a premium azadirachtin-based bioinsecticide offering broad-spectrum control of sucking pests. It combines fast knockdown with repellency, making it an ideal fit for residue-restricted export programs.
