Product Overview

Verticillium lecanii biocontrol tailored for greenhouse and field crops

Seira is engineered for distributors requiring consistent Verticillium lecanii performance on whiteflies, aphids, and soft-scale insects. Fermentation and formulation are optimised to maintain 1×10¹⁰ cfu counts and ensure rapid spore germination even under fluctuating humidity. Both EC and WP options provide stable suspensions, compatibility with soft chemistries, and export-ready documentation that accelerates regulatory clearances.

Dosage & Dilution

Seira application guidance

Seira EC – Verticillium lecanii 1×10¹⁰ cfu/mL

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Greenhouse foliar sprays 400–600 mL 4–6 mL / L Every 5–7 days
Field vegetables & herbs 500–700 mL 5–7 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Misting / fogging systems 300 mL in 80 L water 3.75 mL / L Every 5 days during outbreaks

Seira WP – Verticillium lecanii 1×10¹⁰ cfu/g

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Greenhouse drench 0.5–0.75 kg 5–7.5 g / L Every 10 days
Nursery dip 0.4 kg in 100 L water 4 g / L Single pre-transplant dip
Field sprays 0.6–0.8 kg 6–8 g / L Every 7–10 days

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust volumes according to local application equipment.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Greenhouses and net-houses
  • Open-field vegetables and herbs
  • Ornamental nurseries and floriculture
  • Fruit orchards with whitefly pressure
  • Protected berry production

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most botanical insecticides and soaps
  • Avoid mixing with copper or sulfur fungicides
  • Maintain spray pH between 5.5–7.0
  • Use within 12 hours after mixing

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18 months
  • Store sealed packs at 10–30°C
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
  • Shake or stir product before charging tanks
