Product Overview

Bacillus subtilis for preventive biological suppression of foliar and soil-borne disease

Subtilix is a Bacillus subtilis wettable powder for biological suppression of foliar and root-zone pathogens. It is suited to preventive disease programs that require rapid microbial colonization and metabolite-based antagonism.

Phyllosphere & Rhizosphere

Beneficial Bacillus with strong colonization of the phyllosphere and rhizosphere.

Lipopeptide Antagonism

Produces lipopeptides and enzymes that suppress pathogens.

Preventive & Root-Zone Use

Useful in preventive disease management and root-zone stabilization. Can be applied as foliar spray or root-zone drench.
