Product Overview

Bacillus amyloliquefaciens for lipopeptide-based biological suppression across foliar and root-zone diseases

Elixora™ is a Bacillus amyloliquefaciens wettable powder for preventive suppression of foliar and root-zone pathogens. It is suited to integrated biological disease programs that rely on lipopeptide-producing Bacillus strains.
