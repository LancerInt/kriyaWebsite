Product Overview

Botanical knockdown that keeps spray programs compliant

K-Guard pairs karanjin-rich extracts with advanced emulsification to deliver consistent insect growth regulation in export-grade production systems. Each batch is cold-filtered to stabilize furanoflavonoids and maximize contact efficacy on resistant sucking pests. Manufacturing controls keep solvent residues below international thresholds, enabling low PHI schedules without compromising performance. The EC concentrate and WP dispersible powder give distributors flexibility across humid, arid, and protected environments. Detailed dossiers, SDS, and field protocols ship with every order to simplify market onboarding.

Dosage & Dilution

Label-aligned guidance for karanjin programs

K-Guard 2% EC – Karanjin Emulsifiable Concentrate

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & spices 500–750 mL 2.0–3.0 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Plantation crops 600–900 mL 2.4–3.6 mL / L Every 10–14 days
Protected cultivation 400–600 mL 1.6–2.4 mL / L Every 5–7 days

K-Rix 5% WP – Karanjin Wettable Powder

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vegetables & herbs 350–500 g 1.4–2.0 g / L Every 7–10 days
Ornamentals & nursery 300–450 g 1.2–1.8 g / L Every 5–7 days
Plantations 400–600 g 1.6–2.4 g / L Every 10–14 days

*Assumes spray volumes of 200–250 L per acre for field applications. Adjust water volume to canopy density and sprayer output.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, pulses, cotton, and oilseeds
  • Fruit orchards and plantation crops requiring low residues
  • Protected cultivation including greenhouse and shade-net systems
  • Nursery and ornamental production facilities
  • Organic transition acres seeking botanical rotations

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most biopesticides, spreader-stickers, and low-alkaline fertilizers
  • Avoid tank mixing with strong alkaline materials or Bordeaux blends
  • Maintain spray solution pH between 5.5–7.0 for optimal stability
  • Add last in tank after wettable powders and dispersible granules

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Keep containers tightly closed to prevent solvent loss or moisture uptake
  • Agitate drums or roll bags before dilution to re-suspend active ingredients
