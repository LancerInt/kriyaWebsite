Dosage & Dilution

Label-aligned guidance for karanjin programs

K-Guard – Karanjin EC

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early sucking or chewing pest infestation 400–600 mL/acre 150–200 L Foliar spray with full canopy coverage Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sprays in sequence
Moderate infestation 600–800 mL/acre 180–220 L Foliar spray focusing on tender plant parts Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sprays in sequence

K-Rix – Karanjin 5% WP

Crop Stage Dose per Acre Water Volume per Acre Application Method Application Interval
Early sucking or chewing pest infestation 300–500 g/acre 150–200 L Foliar spray after preparing a uniform suspension Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sprays in sequence
Moderate infestation 500–700 g/acre 180–220 L Foliar spray with continuous agitation Repeat every 7–10 days; 2–3 sprays in sequence

*Assumes spray volumes of 200–250 L per acre for field applications. Adjust water volume to canopy density and sprayer output.

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix flexibility, and storage guidance

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, pulses, cotton, and oilseeds
  • Fruit orchards and plantation crops requiring low residues
  • Protected cultivation including greenhouse and shade-net systems
  • Nursery and ornamental production facilities
  • Organic transition acres seeking botanical rotations

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most neutral-pH spray inputs
  • Avoid strongly alkaline mixes and confirm with a jar test

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Typical shelf life: 24 months in the unopened original container under cool, dry, shaded storage
  • Store sealed in the original pack away from heat, direct sunlight, and moisture
