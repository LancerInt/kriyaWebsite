Product Overview

Customizable essential oil solutions for evolving pest pressure

Admira is Kriya’s modular botanical program that pairs premium essential oils with advanced emulsifiers and dispersants. Each variant is built on standardized active content for regulatory reliability, ensuring batch-to-batch consistency in aroma, potency, and solubility. EC concentrates emulsify instantly in soft or hard water, while WSP powders disperse to a fine suspension with minimal residue. Dedicated QC protocols validate density, viscosity, and active integrity for shipping across climate zones. With aligned documentation and supply planning, distributors can launch differentiated organic pest management portfolios at speed.

Target Pests

Insect and mite spectrum

  • Spider mites and broad mites on vegetables, berries, and ornamentals
  • Aphids, whiteflies, and mealybugs in open-field and protected crops
  • Leafminer larvae and soft-bodied pests sensitive to essential oils
  • Caterpillar eggs and neonates through contact and fumigant action
  • Fungal leaf spots including Alternaria, Colletotrichum, and powdery mildew suppression

Program Fit

Use sites, tank mix guidance, and storage best practices

map Use Sites

  • Open-field vegetables, spices, and floriculture
  • Fruit orchards, vineyards, and nut plantations
  • Protected cultivation, greenhouse ornamentals, and nurseries
  • High-value medicinal and aromatic crops
  • Organic and residue-sensitive export programs

science Compatibility

  • Mixable with most foliar fertilizers, biostimulants, and biological insecticides
  • Conduct jar test before combining with copper, sulfur, or strong alkaline products
  • Maintain spray solution pH between 5.5–7.0 for optimal stability
  • Introduce Admira last in the spray tank after dispersible powders and granules

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Shelf life: 24 months in unopened original containers
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct heat and sunlight
  • Keep containers tightly sealed to preserve aroma integrity
  • Agitate drums or bulk packs before decanting for uniform active dispersion
