Product Overview

Ampelomyces quisqualis for residue-conscious powdery mildew programs

Neuvita delivers high-count Ampelomyces quisqualis in OD and WP formats to combat powdery mildew across horticulture, vines, and protected crops. Carefully controlled fermentation protects spore viability, while formulation chemistry ensures stability in spray tanks and misting systems. Neuvita parasitises powdery mildew colonies, reducing sporulation and slowing disease cycles without introducing residue concerns for export markets.

Dosage & Dilution

Neuvita application guidance

Neuvita OD – Ampelomyces quisqualis 1 × 109 CFU/mL

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vineyards & orchards 500–700 mL 5–7 mL / L Every 7–10 days
Greenhouse vegetables 400–600 mL 4–6 mL / L Every 7 days under pressure
Misting / ULV systems 300 mL in 80 L water 3.75 mL / L Every 5–7 days

Neuvita WP – Ampelomyces quisqualis 1 × 109 CFU/g

Application Focus Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval
Vineyard and orchard sprays 0.6–0.8 kg 6–8 g / L Every 10 days
Greenhouse fogging 0.4 kg in 80 L water 5 g / L Every 5–7 days
Field cucurbits 0.7–0.9 kg 7–9 g / L Every 7–10 days

*Per-liter guidance assumes a 100 L spray volume per acre. Adjust spray volume to canopy size and local equipment.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Vineyards and trellised orchards
  • Greenhouse vegetables and ornamentals
  • Berry tunnels and net-houses
  • Cucurbit fields under IPM
  • Nursery propagation houses

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most botanical fungicides and biostimulants
  • Avoid tank mixing with sulfur or copper products
  • Maintain spray pH between 5.5–7.0
  • Add as the final component in the spray tank

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 18 months
  • Store between 10–25°C away from sunlight
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent moisture ingress
  • Use prepared spray solutions within 12 hours
