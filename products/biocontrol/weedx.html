Product Overview

Contact herbicide performance with organic assurance

WeedX harnesses medium-chain triglycerides derived from natural plant oils to deliver rapid burn-down of troublesome weeds without compromising organic certifications. The emulsifiable concentrate is engineered for consistent droplet spread and leaf penetration, breaking down cuticles and desiccating tissues within hours. Growers can integrate WeedX into stale seedbed, directed spray, and orchard floor programs to reduce reliance on synthetic herbicides. The formulation maintains stability across a wide temperature range, mixes quickly with water, and leaves no soil residue. Distributors receive full regulatory documentation, SDS, and marketing collateral to accelerate onboarding across markets.

Dosage & Dilution

Label-aligned guidance for WeedX EC

WeedX EC – Botanical Fatty Acid Herbicide

Application Target Dosage per Acre Product per Liter of Water* Application Interval Guidance
Emerging annual weeds (1–4 leaf) 1.5–2.5 gal (5.7–9.5 L) 30 mL / L (3% v/v) Repeat 7–14 days as needed Broadcast in 40–60 gal spray volume for uniform coverage.
Established annuals & biennials 3.0–4.5 gal (11.4–17.0 L) 60 mL / L (6% v/v) Reapply 7–10 days after first treatment Increase spray volume to 50–80 gal per acre and ensure wetting agents.
Perennials, suckers, tough regrowth 6.0–7.5 gal (22.7–28.4 L) 90 mL / L (9% v/v) Re-treat regrowth every 5–7 days Direct spray to green tissue; avoid drift to desirable plants.

*Dilution guidance derived from SUPPRESS® Herbicide EC label (caprylic/capric acid) for equivalent active delivery.

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Organic vegetables, herbs, and leafy greens
  • Vineyards, orchards, and plantation crops
  • Row crop field edges, in-row directed sprays, and stale seedbeds
  • Greenhouse benches, nursery containers, and hardscapes
  • Non-crop areas within production facilities

science Compatibility

  • Mix with approved adjuvants or wetting agents to improve coverage
  • Do not tank mix with copper or sulfur without prior jar test
  • Avoid alkaline water; buffer to pH 5.5–6.5 for optimal stability
  • Apply alone when temperatures exceed 32°C to minimize phytotoxicity

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Unopened shelf life: 24 months from manufacturing
  • Store between 5–30°C away from direct sunlight
  • Keep containers sealed to prevent solvent evaporation
  • Agitate drums or totes before decanting for uniform concentration
