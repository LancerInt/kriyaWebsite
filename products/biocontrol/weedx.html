Product Overview

Contact herbicide performance with organic assurance

WeedX™ is a fatty-acid-based botanical contact pesticide intended for exposed soft-bodied pests and eggs. Performance depends strongly on direct spray contact and is best in early infestations with good canopy access.

Mode of Action

Contact-active fatty acids solubilize cuticular lipids and disrupt cell membranes, causing leakage, desiccation, and rapid loss of mobility in exposed pests

Concentration Dilution Rate Application Rate Application Method Application Interval
3% – Light weed pressure 30 ml/L Thorough contact spray to actively growing weeds Broadcast, shielded, or spot spray; contact herbicide 10–15 days depending on weed growth

Program Fit

Use sites, compatibility, and storage

map Use Sites

  • Organic vegetables, herbs, and leafy greens
  • Vineyards, orchards, and plantation crops
  • Row crop field edges, in-row directed sprays, and stale seedbeds
  • Greenhouse benches, nursery containers, and hardscapes
  • Non-crop areas within production facilities

science Compatibility

  • Compatible with most neutral-pH spray inputs
  • Avoid strongly alkaline mixes and confirm with a jar test

inventory Shelf Life & Storage

  • Typical shelf life: 24 months in the unopened original container under cool, dry, shaded storage
  • Store sealed in the original pack away from heat, direct sunlight, and moisture
