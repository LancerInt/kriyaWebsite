Manufacturing overview

From lab-scale to commercial supply

Kriya offers a full suite of contract services for companies developing natural crop care solutions. Our facilities combine extraction, fermentation, formulation, and packaging with traceable QA/QC workflows.

Our teams work across botanical and microbial actives, converting R&D prototypes into market-ready formulations. With in-house analytical labs, regulatory specialists, and dedicated production managers, we shorten commercialization timelines while ensuring compliance across global markets.
