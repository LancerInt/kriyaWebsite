Platform Overview

Cross-platform active delivery and protection

Karyo is the delivery and protection layer of the Kriya stack. Unlike Wynn and Microvate, Karyo is not restricted to microbial systems. It is built for both microbial and botanical actives and can support encapsulated single-actives, multi-actives, and hybrid combinations within structured agricultural delivery systems. Karyo focuses on how sensitive actives are protected, combined, and translated into usable field-facing product architectures.

Core Positioning

Active performance depends on delivery architecture

Karyo is built on a simple principle: active performance depends on delivery architecture. The value of an active is determined not only by what it is, but by how it is protected, combined, and delivered under real agricultural conditions.

Role in the Stack

What Karyo owns and what it does not

Owns

  • Encapsulation systems
  • Active protection
  • Multi-active combination logic
  • Microbial plus botanical co-delivery
  • Carrier and matrix design
  • Delivery architecture
  • Format flexibility
  • Shelf-life and application compatibility

Does Not Own

  • Microbial discovery
  • Strain selection
  • Fermentation process engineering
  • Upstream microbial stabilization science
  • Point-of-use readiness triggering
